Tips & Info

Good to Know

Everything you need before you go.

Before You Go
🛂
Documents

Keep your passport accessible and save a digital copy in your email. Check entry requirements at least 2 weeks before your trip.

💵
Money & Cards

Notify your bank before you leave. Most places take cards but carry some local currency for markets, small vendors, and tips.

📱
Phone Plan

Check if your carrier offers an international day pass before you go. A local SIM on arrival is always a solid backup option.

⏰
Time Zone

Your personalized guide will include the local time zone so you can set alarms and plan calls from home ahead of time.

While You're There
🧴
Sunscreen

Pack reef-safe SPF 50+ if your trip includes water activities. Reapply often and don't skip lip balm with SPF.

🌤
Weather

Your personalized guide will have destination-specific info. Light layers always come in handy for AC and cooler evenings.

🔒
Safety

Stick to busy, well-lit areas and leave valuables in the hotel safe. Your advisor will include any destination-specific safety tips.

🚌
Getting Around

Transportation options vary by destination. Your advisor will outline the best ways to get around, including rideshare tips.
