The Process

Here's How It Works

No guessing, no surprises. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you reach out to the moment you're packed and ready to go.

1

Fill Out the Inquiry

Tell us about your dream trip. Where you want to go, when, who's coming, and your budget. It's free and takes just a few minutes.

2

1-on-1 Consultation

We'll hop on a call to talk through your vision, answer your questions, and map out the right plan for you. No commitment at this stage. Just a real conversation.

3

We Get to Work

If you're ready to move forward, you'll receive a booking link to pay the research fee. Once that's in, we handle everything.

Heads up: The research fee covers the time and expertise it takes to build your perfect itinerary. It's paid only after your consultation, so you always know exactly what you're getting before you commit.
